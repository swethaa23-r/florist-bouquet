Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM local time. Any orders placed after 2:00 PM will be delivered the following business day.
How can I make my flowers last longer?
To maximize the life of your arrangement, change the water every 2 days, trim the stems at a 45-degree angle before placing them in water, and keep them away from direct sunlight, drafts, and fruit. We also include a packet of flower food with every order!
Do you do custom arrangements for weddings?
Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke wedding floristry. Please visit our Services page or contact us directly to schedule a consultation with our lead floral designer.
What is your return/refund policy?
Because flowers are perishable, we do not accept returns. However, if your arrangement arrives damaged or you are unsatisfied with the quality, please contact us within 24 hours with a photo, and we will gladly replace it or issue a refund.
